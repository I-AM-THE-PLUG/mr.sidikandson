Our Story

Three Decades of Industrial Excellence

Founded in 1990 by Mr. Mohammed Sidik, M. SIDIK & SONS COMPANY LIMITED began as a modest electrical contracting firm serving local businesses in Accra.

Through unwavering commitment to quality workmanship and client satisfaction, we've grown into one of Ghana's most respected multi-disciplinary industrial conglomerates. Today, we proudly serve clients across West Africa in construction, engineering, mining logistics, and strategic consulting.

Despite our growth, we maintain the same family values that built our reputation: integrity, reliability, and a genuine passion for helping our clients succeed. Every project we undertake reflects our dedication to excellence and our promise to deliver solutions that stand the test of time.
